Our Mission

Our mission is to keep the generations-old familiar neighborhood convenience while bringing more local and sustainable foods to our community. We aim to keep our great relationship with our customers but to increase our relationships and support of local farmers, fisherman, lobstermen, artisans and merchants, bringing more healthy and sustainable options to our patrons. 

We aim to provide healthy, nutritious food for our customers. We hope to build a deep relationship with the community and customer base in order to understand and meet their needs. We provide delicious fresh foods prepared in-house and by local vendors. We have established relationships with local fishermen and lobstermen. The market is now offering more local farm products; vegetable, dairy and meat. 

In the spirit of giving back to the community and supporting local NGOs, we are committed to supporting local charitable organizations like the Falmouth Land Trust. We are also thrilled to be a refreshment stop each year during the Maine Marathon held in October and ask our neighbors to stop by the market and cheer on the runners and offer them refreshments as they run the race of their life!
